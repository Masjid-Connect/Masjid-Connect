Managing Announcements

Keep your community informed with timely updates and notices.

How to Add an Announcement

  1. Click Announcements in the left sidebar under "Content".
  2. Click the "Add Announcement" button in the top-right corner.
  3. Select your Mosque from the dropdown list.
  4. Type a clear, short Title — this is what people see first (e.g., "Jumu'ah Khutbah Topic This Week").
  5. Write the full Body of your announcement. Keep it concise — a few sentences is ideal.
  6. Choose a Priority:
    • Normal — for regular updates (weekly reminders, general news)
    • Urgent — for time-sensitive notices (Janazah prayers, emergency closures)
  7. Set an Expiry Date if the announcement should automatically disappear (optional). Leave it blank if it should stay until you remove it.
  8. Click "Save" at the bottom of the page.
Push notifications are sent automatically. When you save a new announcement, everyone subscribed to your mosque who has announcement notifications turned on will receive a push notification on their phone. Urgent announcements are delivered with high priority so they appear immediately.
Keep titles short and specific. "Mosque Closed on Monday" is better than "Important Notice Regarding Closure". Remember — the title is what appears in the push notification, so make it clear at a glance.
Only use Urgent priority for genuinely time-sensitive matters. Urgent announcements send high-priority notifications that may bypass Do Not Disturb. If everything is marked urgent, community members will start ignoring notifications.

Editing an Existing Announcement

  1. Click Announcements in the left sidebar.
  2. Find the announcement you want to edit — use the search box or scroll through the list.
  3. Click on the title of the announcement to open it.
  4. Make your changes and click "Save".

Deleting an Announcement

  1. Open the announcement you want to delete.
  2. Scroll to the bottom and click "Delete".
  3. You'll be asked to confirm — click "Yes, I'm sure".
Deleted announcements cannot be recovered. If you're unsure, set an expiry date instead of deleting.

Quick Reference

Field Required? What to Enter
Mosque Yes Select your mosque from the list
Title Yes A short, clear heading (e.g., "Weekend Class Cancelled")
Body Yes The full announcement text — keep it brief and clear
Priority Yes Normal (default) or Urgent (for emergencies only)
Expires At No Date/time when the announcement should disappear automatically
