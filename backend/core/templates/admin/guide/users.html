Users & Subscribers

See who is connected to your mosque and manage admin access.

Viewing Subscribers

When a community member subscribes to your mosque in the app, they appear in the Subscriptions list. Each subscription shows their notification preferences.

  1. Click Subscriptions in the left sidebar under "Users".
  2. You'll see which users are subscribed to which mosques, and what notifications they want:
    • Prayer notifications — reminders before jama'ah times
    • Announcement notifications — new announcements
    • Event notifications — new events and lessons
Subscribers manage their own notification preferences in the app. You can see their choices here, but they control what notifications they receive.

Managing Mosque Admins

Mosque Admins are people who can manage content (announcements, events, prayer times) for a specific mosque.

  1. Click Mosque Admins in the left sidebar under "Mosques".
  2. To add a new admin, click "Add Mosque Admin".
  3. Select the User (they must have an account already) and the Mosque.
  4. Choose their Role:
    • Admin — can manage announcements, events, and prayer times
    • Super Admin — can do everything above, plus manage other admins and view donations
  5. Click "Save".
Start new admins with the "Admin" role. Only give "Super Admin" to trusted individuals who need to manage finances and other admins.

Push Tokens

Push Tokens are technical records that allow the app to send notifications to specific devices. You don't need to do anything with these — they are managed automatically.

If you see push tokens in the sidebar, you can ignore them. They are listed for technical support purposes only.

Viewing User Accounts

  1. Click Users in the left sidebar.
  2. You can search by name or email to find a specific person.
  3. Click on a user to see their details — name, email, and when they joined.
Be careful when editing user accounts. Changing someone's email or password can lock them out of the app. Only do this if the person has asked you to.
