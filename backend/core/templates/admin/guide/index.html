Admin User Guide

Everything you need to manage your mosque — simply and confidently.

Welcome!

This guide is designed for mosque administrators — imams, board members, and volunteers who manage announcements, events, prayer times, and donations through this admin panel. No technical knowledge is required.

You can return to this guide at any time from the Help & Guides section in the left sidebar.

What Can You Do Here?

📣
Announcements
Post community announcements — from weekly updates to urgent Janazah notices.
 📅
Events & Lessons
Add lessons, lectures, youth programs, sisters' circles, and community events.
 🕐
Prayer Times
View and update daily jama'ah (congregation) times for your mosque.
 💰
Donations & Gift Aid
View donation records, manage Gift Aid declarations, and generate HMRC claims.
 👥
Users & Subscribers
See who is subscribed to your mosque and their notification preferences.
 ❓
FAQs
Quick answers to the most common questions about using this admin panel.
 🔧
Troubleshooting
Something not working? Find solutions to common issues here.

Your Role

As a mosque administrator, you have the ability to:

Role What You Can Do
Admin Create and edit announcements, events, and prayer times for your mosque
Super Admin Everything above, plus manage other admins, view donations, and generate Gift Aid claims
If you're not sure which role you have, ask the person who set up your account. Your role is shown in the Mosque Admins section.
