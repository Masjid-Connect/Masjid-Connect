# Mosque Connect — Project Definition

## Purpose

A premium mobile app **exclusively for The Salafi Masjid, Birmingham** — prayer time notifications, event/lesson listings, and community announcements.

## Scope

- **Single-mosque app.** No mosque selection, no discovery, no multi-mosque subscriptions, no SaaS.
- Congregants of The Salafi Masjid are the only users.
- English only for MVP. Arabic/RTL not shipping.

## Philosophy

- **God-tier, not SaaS.** Serene, premium, rooted in Islamic geometric art and calligraphic tradition.
- **Offline-first.** Prayer times, announcements, and events are always viewable without a connection.
- **Admin-first usability.** Non-technical volunteers (imams, board members) must be able to post an announcement in under 60 seconds.
