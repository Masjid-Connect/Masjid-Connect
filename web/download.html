Getting Started

1

Download & Open

Install the app from the App Store or Google Play. Open it and you'll be greeted with a brief welcome screen explaining what the app does.

2

Allow Location Access

The app needs your location to calculate accurate prayer times. This data never leaves your device — it's used only for local calculations.

3

Set Your Notifications

Choose which prayers you want reminders for and how far in advance (5, 10, 15, or 30 minutes). Enable notifications for announcements and upcoming lessons.

4

You're Connected

That's it. Prayer times update automatically. Announcements arrive when the masjid posts them. Events appear in your calendar. Never miss a prayer, never miss a lesson.

Common Questions

Do I need an account to use the app?

No. Prayer times and browsing announcements all work without an account. You only need to sign up if you want to subscribe to a masjid for push notifications.

Does the app work offline?

Yes. Prayer times calculate locally from your coordinates when you're offline. Previously loaded announcements and events are cached on your device.

Which calculation method is used?

The app uses the Umm Al-Qura method, which is the standard used by Masjid al-Haram in Makkah. Prayer times are fetched from the Aladhan API when online, with adhan-js as an offline fallback.

Is my location data shared?

No. Your location is used only on your device to calculate prayer times. It is never sent to our servers or any third party.

Will there ever be ads or paid features?

No. This app is a sadaqah jaariyah — an ongoing charity. It will always be free, with no ads, no in-app purchases, and no premium tiers.

Can other mosques use this app?

The app is currently built for The Salafi Masjid, Birmingham. If you're interested in having your mosque added to the platform, please contact us at info@salafimasjid.app.
