Where your donation goes

All donations go directly to The Salafi Masjid, Birmingham — part of Salafi Bookstore and Islamic Centre (Registered Charity No. 1083080).

Masjid Maintenance

Utilities, repairs, cleaning, and keeping the masjid open for all five daily prayers.

Education & Da'wah

Weekly lessons, Quran circles, youth programmes, and da'wah materials.

Community Services

Supporting families, Ramadan iftars, Eid celebrations, and community welfare.
